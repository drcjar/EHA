The Environment and Health Atlas

A collection of maps illustrating the geographical distribution of disease risk and environmental agents

The atlas provides interactive maps of geographical variations for a range of health outcomes and environmental agents at a small-area (neighbourhood) scale in England and Wales.

These maps have been developed as a resource for the public, researchers and anyone working in public health and public health policy to better understand the geographic distribution of environmental agents and health outcomes in England and Wales. It is important to note that we are not attempting to make direct causal links between the mapped environmental agents and health outcomes.
